Featured Artists

Areen Zainab

The Narrative Surrealist

Merging dreamlike imagery with intricate symbolism, she crafts visually poetic compositions that blur the line between reality and fantasy. Her artwork often portrays mysterious figures, imaginary landscapes, and surreal juxtapositions that provoke curiosity and introspection.

Mahum Hamid

The Experimental Mixed-Media Artist

An innovator in texture and material exploration, she seamlessly blends painting, collage, and found objects to create immersive, tactile works. Her art reflects a deep interest in cultural history, personal narratives, and environmental concerns. By layering organic and industrial materials, she builds compositions that challenge traditional notions of form and meaning.

Abeer Jawad

The Contemporary Minimalist

With a refined and meditative approach to art, she focuses on simplicity, balance, and spatial harmony. Her work often features geometric shapes, muted tones, and subtle textures, inviting viewers to engage in quiet contemplation. Inspired by nature and architecture, she reduces forms to their essence, creating a serene and introspective atmosphere in her paintings and sculptures.

Maryum Fasih

The Visionary Expressionist

A passionate artist known for her bold, emotive brushstrokes and a vivid color palette, she captures raw human emotions and psychological depth in her paintings. Her works often explore themes of identity, memory, and personal transformation, blending abstract forms with figurative elements.
